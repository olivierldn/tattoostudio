Mara brings photographic depth to skin. Trained in Berlin and refined across seven years of dedicated black and grey work, her pieces blur the line between tattooing and fine art portraiture. She specialises in hyper-realistic portraits, botanical studies, and anatomical subjects — each one a permanent study in shadow and light.

Kai Noda

Fine Line
& Geometry

Precision is Kai's language. With a background in architectural draughting and five years of dedicated fine line practice, Kai approaches every piece as a structural problem — one solved with a single needle and unwavering patience. Geometric mandalas, sacred geometry, and delicate botanical illustrations are his signatures; clean, considered, and built to age beautifully.

Felix Sol

Neo-
Traditional

Felix believes tattoos should be bold enough to last a lifetime — visually and structurally. Drawing from classic American and Japanese traditions while pushing them into contemporary territory, his work is defined by strong outlines, rich shading, and imagery that tells a complete story at a glance. If Mara paints and Kai drafts, Felix illustrates.

How it Works

The Process

01
Consultation

We start with a conversation — your ideas, reference images, placement, and story. No booking required for an initial chat.

02
Custom Design

Your artist creates a bespoke design. You'll review and refine until it's exactly right before we commit to skin.

03
The Session

In our calm, private studio space. Music of your choice, breaks when needed, and absolute focus on the work.

04
Aftercare

Full written aftercare instructions, a follow-up check at 4 weeks, and a free touch-up within 3 months if needed.
